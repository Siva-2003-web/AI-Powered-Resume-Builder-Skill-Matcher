# Sample Job Descriptions for Testing

Here are some sample job descriptions you can use to test the **Skill Gap Analysis** and **Job Matcher** features.

## 1. Senior Frontend Developer (React)

**Role:** Senior Frontend Developer
**Company:** TechFlow Solutions
**Location:** Remote

**About the Role:**
We are looking for an experienced Frontend Developer to lead our UI/UX team. You will be building responsive web applications using modern React patterns.

**Requirements:**

- 5+ years of experience with **JavaScript** and **TypeScript**.
- Expert knowledge of **React.js** (Hooks, Context API).
- Experience with state management tools like **Redux Toolkit** or Zustand.
- Proficiency in **Tailwind CSS** and responsive design.
- Familiarity with **Next.js** or **Vite** build tools.
- Experience with **REST APIs** and **GraphQL**.
- Knowledge of testing frameworks like **Jest** or **Cypress**.

**Nice to Have:**

- Experience with WebGL or Three.js.
- CI/CD pipeline knowledge.

## 2. Backend Engineer (Node.js)

**Role:** Backend Engineer
**Company:** CloudScale Systems
**Location:** New York, NY

**Job Description:**
Join our backend team to build scalable microservices. You will design and implement high-performance APIs.

**Key Responsibilities:**

- Develop RESTful APIs using **Node.js** and **Express**.
- Design and optimize **MongoDB** database schemas.
- Implement authentication using **JWT** and **OAuth**.
- Write efficient, reusable, and testable code.
- collaborate with frontend developers to integrate user-facing elements.

**Technical Skills:**

- Strong proficiency in **Node.js** and server-side JavaScript.
- Deep understanding of **NoSQL databases** (MongoDB).
- Experience with **Docker** and containerization.
- Knowledge of AWS services (EC2, S3, Lambda).
- Familiarity with **Redis** for caching.

## 3. Full Stack MERN Developer

**Role:** Full Stack Developer
**Company:** StartupX
**Location:** San Francisco, CA

**Overview:**
We are a fast-growing startup looking for a Full Stack Developer who can handle both client-side and server-side development.

**Requirements:**
